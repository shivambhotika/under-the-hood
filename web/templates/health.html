How to use this table
The most investable tools sit in the top-left of the signal space: high health score AND rising momentum. That means a tool that is growing quickly AND safe to build on.

Health Score factors: release cadence, known vulnerabilities, transitive dependency count, maintainer concentration, and license type.

Momentum comes from adoption growth across tracked repositories.

A tool can be Healthy but Stable (established infrastructure) or Declining Health but Rising (growing fast but accumulating tech debt). The intersection tells you the most.
