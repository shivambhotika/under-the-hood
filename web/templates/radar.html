How to use The Radar

The Radar surfaces tools in the pre-commercial window — after they have proven real adoption but before a company has been built around them. This is historically the highest-value discovery window for investors.

What to look for:

  • Enterprise adopters (even 1-2) signal real-world validation
  • Contributors with research credentials or strong community reputation
  • A clear problem being solved (check the tool description)
  • No known VC funding or commercial entity yet

What this is NOT:

  • A prediction that these tools will succeed
  • A complete picture (private repos are invisible)
  • A recommendation to invest in anything
